Our Legacy of Luxury

The Daniel Auto Dealer Difference

Founded in 1980 by auto maker Daniel Aduayi, Daniel Auto Dealer began as a small family-run dealership with a big dream: to redefine luxury car buying. What started as a modest operation with just five vehicles has grown into one of the most respected names in premium automotive retail.

Our Philosophy

We believe that buying a luxury vehicle should be as exceptional as the vehicle itself. Our approach combines old-world hospitality with cutting-edge technology to create a seamless, personalized experience.

Our Inventory

Our carefully curated selection represents the pinnacle of automotive engineering. As an authorized dealer for 15+ luxury brands, we maintain an inventory of 150-200 vehicles at any time.

Brand Partnerships

Our relationships with manufacturers give us access to exclusive models and custom configurations unavailable through most dealers.

Our Team

Our 45-person team includes factory-trained technicians and sales consultants with an average of 12 years industry experience.
