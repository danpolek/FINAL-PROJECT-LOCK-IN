Our Legacy of Luxury

The Daniel Auto Dealer Difference

Founded in 1980 by auto maker Daniel Aduayi, Daniel Auto Dealer began as a small family-run dealership with a big dream: to redefine luxury car buying. What started as a modest operation with just five vehicles has grown into one of the most respected names in premium automotive retail, with showrooms across three states and an international empire.

Our Philosophy

We believe that buy a luxury vehicle should be as awesome as the vehicle itself. Our approach combines old-world hospitality with cutting-edge technology to create a seamless, personalized experience for every client. Unlike typical dealerships, we focus on building lifelong relationships rather than making one-time sales.

Our Inventory

Our carefully curated selection represents the pinnacle of automotive engineering and design. As an authorized dealer for over 15 luxury brands including Lamborghini, Ferrari, Porsche, Rolls-Royce, and Bentley, we maintain an inventory of 150-200 vehicles at any time. Each undergoes our rigorous 150-point certification process to ensure it meets our exacting standards.

Brand Partnerships

Our longstanding relationships with manufacturers give us access to exclusive models, limited editions, and custom configurations unavailable through most dealers. We're proud to be one of only 12 dealers nationwide with Ferrari's "Prestige" designation and Lamborghini's "Ad Personam" customization program.

Our Team

Our 45-person team includes factory-trained technicians, master detailers, and sales consultants with an average of 12 years industry experience. Many of our staff hold manufacturer certifications from BMW, Mercedes-Benz, and Porsche, ensuring expert knowledge of every vehicle we sell.

Our Journey Through Time

1980

Daniel Aduayi opens the original dealership in downtown Chicago with just 5 pre-owned luxury vehicles

1987

Earns first manufacturer certification (Mercedes-Benz) and becomes an authorized dealer

1995

Opens second location in Miami, specializing in exotic and high-performance vehicles

2002

Launches the industry-leading 150-point certification program for pre-owned vehicles

2010

Receives Ferrari's "Prestige" designation for exceptional sales and service

2018

Expands to Beverly Hills location, tripling showroom space and adding concierge services

2025

Celebrates 5,000 vehicles sold with record customer satisfaction scores
