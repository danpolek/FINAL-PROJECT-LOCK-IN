Welcome to Daniel Auto Dealer! Since 1980, we have been proud to serve customers with high-quality, premium vehicles and exceptional customer service. Whether you're looking for a brand-new luxury model or a certified pre-owned vehicle, we’re here to help you make the right choice.

Use the form on this page to contact us directly. Our friendly team is ready to assist you with any inquiries regarding our current inventory, pricing details, or upcoming promotions. If you’re not sure which vehicle fits your lifestyle, we’re happy to provide recommendations based on your needs.

Need help with financing options? Our financing experts are on hand to walk you through car loan applications, lease agreements, and payment plans tailored just for you. We work with top lenders to ensure competitive rates for all our customers.

Looking to schedule a test drive? Mention your preferred model and time in the subject box and we’ll get back to you within 24 hours. All appointments are confirmed via email or phone for your convenience.
