Our Legacy

40+ Years of Automotive Excellence

Founded in 1980 by Daniel Aduayi, Daniel Auto Dealer began as a small family-owned business with a passion for quality vehicles. What started as a modest used car lot has grown into one of the most respected dealerships in the region.

Our founder's vision was simple: treat every customer like family and never compromise on quality. These principles still guide us today as we continue to serve generations of satisfied customers.

We specialize in luxury imports, high-performance vehicles, and premium SUVs, with each vehicle undergoing our rigorous 150-point inspection process before being offered to our clients.

In our inventory you'll find high-class cars with extraordinary luxury. We offer vehicles to fit any family, whether it's a family of 3 or 6. We're proud to be an authorized dealer for multiple brands including Lamborghini, Ferrari, Porsche and many more.
